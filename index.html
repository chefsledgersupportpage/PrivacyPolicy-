1. Introduction

Welcome to Chef's Ledger. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your data is handled. This Privacy Policy explains what information Chef's Ledger collects, how it is used, stored, and protected.

By using Chef's Ledger, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Chef's Ledger collects and stores the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Inventory Data: item names, quantities, units, prices, categories, expiration dates, minimum stock levels, preferred stores, notes, custom attributes.
  • Group Management: group names, descriptions, settings, members, permissions, shared inventory data.
  • Shopping Lists: list items, list names, list organization.
  • Expenses & Usage Tracking: usage records, expense entries, purchase history, price tracking data.
  • User Profile: optional name, optional email address, preferences, notification settings.
  • Receipt Data: scanned receipt images (processed locally) and extracted item/price data.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Location (Optional): used only for finding nearby grocery stores; not stored permanently; can be disabled in iOS Settings.
  • Camera Access (Optional): used only for scanning receipts; photos processed locally and not stored unless you save them.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps Time Zone API: sends location coordinates solely to determine timezone for statistics; Google receives only coordinates; feature is optional.
  • Google Places API: sends location coordinates solely to find nearby grocery stores; Google receives only coordinates; feature is optional.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Chef's Ledger does NOT collect, track, or store:

  • Analytics data
  • Advertising IDs
  • Device identifiers (except iCloud sync identifiers)
  • Biometric data
  • Contact information outside what you manually enter
  • Photo library contents (unless you save a receipt)
  • Network information
  • Crash reports
  • Purchase or financial transaction data
  • Social media data
  • Browsing history

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Primary Uses

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • App Functionality: inventory management, group collaboration, shopping lists, expense tracking, statistics, expiration reminders, low-stock notifications.
  • Data Sync (Optional): syncing data across your devices via iCloud if you are signed in.
  • Location Services (Optional): finding nearby grocery stores, determining timezone accuracy.
  • Receipt Scanning (Optional): extracting item and price data locally.

4.2 What We Do NOT Do

  • Sell your data
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Share your data with analytics services
  • Use your data for machine learning or AI training
  • Send your data to external servers (other than optional iCloud sync)

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Primary Storage: Local Device

  • All data stored locally on your iPhone or iPad within the secure app sandbox.
  • Protected by iOS encryption; data remains on device unless you sync.

Optional Storage: iCloud

  • If signed into iCloud, data can sync to your personal iCloud account.
  • Stored in your iCloud storage with end-to-end encryption.
  • You can disable iCloud sync any time via iOS Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Chef's Ledger.

5.2 Security Measures

  • iOS sandboxing
  • Device-level encryption
  • No network transmission unless you enable iCloud sync
  • Local processing of receipts and data
  • No backend servers operated by us

5.3 Data Retention

  • Data stays on your device until you delete it.
  • Deleting the app removes local data.
  • iCloud data remains until you remove it from iCloud Settings.
  • You can export data any time from Profile ‚Üí Export Data.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

6.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

Chef's Ledger does not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

6.2 Third-Party Services

Google APIs (Optional):

  • Google Maps Time Zone API: optional timezone lookup; only coordinates sent; Google Privacy Policy applies.
  • Google Places API: optional store search; only coordinates sent; Google Privacy Policy applies.

iCloud/CloudKit (Optional): Apple may store your data within your personal iCloud account; Apple Privacy Policy applies.

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law (subpoena, court order, protecting rights/safety, mergers/acquisitions). Because we do not operate servers, there is no central data to disclose.
